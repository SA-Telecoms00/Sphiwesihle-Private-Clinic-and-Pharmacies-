Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and healthcare offerings.

Do I need to make an appointment?
While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to minimize your waiting time. You can book online through our website, call us at 071 970 9788, or send us a WhatsApp message.
What are your operating hours?
We are open Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 17:00, and Saturdays and Public Holidays from 08:00 to 13:00. We are closed on Sundays.
Do you accept medical aid?
Yes, we accept most major medical aids. Please contact us to confirm if your specific medical aid is accepted. We also offer affordable cash rates for those without medical aid.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, debit cards, credit cards, and mobile payments. Online payments can also be made through our secure payment portal when booking appointments.
Do you provide emergency services?
We provide immediate attention for urgent cases during our operating hours. For life-threatening emergencies, please call emergency services or visit the nearest hospital emergency room.
