Our Healthcare Services

Comprehensive medical services designed to meet all your healthcare needs with excellence, compassion, and professionalism. From preventive care to chronic management, we're here for every step of your health journey.

Primary Healthcare

Our primary healthcare services form the foundation of your medical care. We provide general consultations, accurate diagnoses, and effective treatments for a wide range of health conditions.

Whether you're dealing with a common cold, need a routine check-up, or require ongoing health monitoring, our experienced healthcare professionals are here to provide personalized care tailored to your needs.

  • General consultations and check-ups
  • Comprehensive health assessments
  • Diagnosis and treatment planning
  • Health education and counseling
  • Referral services when needed
Book Consultation

Family Planning

We offer confidential and comprehensive reproductive healthcare services to help individuals and couples make informed decisions about family planning. Our services are designed to be respectful, private, and tailored to your unique needs.

Our experienced healthcare providers offer guidance on various contraceptive methods, family planning counseling, and reproductive health education in a supportive and non-judgmental environment.

  • Contraceptive counseling and methods
  • Family planning education
  • Pre-conception counseling
  • Fertility awareness methods
  • Confidential and private consultations
Book Appointment

Ante-natal Care

Our comprehensive antenatal care services ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby throughout pregnancy. We provide regular monitoring, screenings, and support to ensure a healthy pregnancy journey.

From the first trimester through delivery, our team of experienced healthcare professionals will guide you through every stage of your pregnancy with personalized care and attention.

  • Regular pregnancy check-ups
  • Ultrasound referrals and monitoring
  • Nutritional guidance and counseling
  • Screening for pregnancy complications
  • Birth planning and preparation
Schedule Ante-natal Visit

Intra-natal Care

Our intra-natal care services provide professional labour and delivery support with experienced healthcare providers who prioritize the safety and comfort of both mother and baby.

We offer comprehensive care during childbirth, including pain management, continuous monitoring, and emotional support to ensure a positive birthing experience.

  • Labour monitoring and support
  • Pain management options
  • Delivery assistance
  • Emergency obstetric care
  • Newborn initial assessment
Learn More

Post-partum Care

Our post-partum care services focus on the recovery of mothers and the comprehensive care of newborns during the critical weeks following delivery. We provide support for both physical and emotional well-being.

From breastfeeding support to newborn health checks, our team ensures that both mother and baby receive the care and guidance needed for a healthy start.

  • Mother's recovery monitoring
  • Newborn health assessments
  • Breastfeeding support
  • Post-partum depression screening
  • Family planning counseling
Book Post-partum Check

Immunizations

We provide comprehensive immunization services for both children and adults, following the recommended vaccination schedule to protect against preventable diseases.

Our clinic maintains proper vaccine storage and follows strict protocols to ensure the safety and effectiveness of every vaccination administered.

  • Childhood vaccinations (EPI schedule)
  • Adult immunizations
  • Travel vaccines
  • Flu shots and seasonal vaccines
  • Vaccination records and reminders
Schedule Vaccination

IMCI Child Health

The Integrated Management of Childhood Illnesses (IMCI) approach ensures comprehensive care for children, addressing their health needs holistically from prevention to treatment.

Our pediatric services focus on early detection, proper treatment, and prevention of common childhood illnesses to ensure healthy growth and development.

  • Growth monitoring and development
  • Treatment of common childhood illnesses
  • Nutritional assessment and counseling
  • Deworming programs
  • Child health education for parents
Book Pediatric Visit

Chronic Disease Management

We provide ongoing care and support for patients living with chronic conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and other long-term health conditions.

Our chronic disease management program includes regular monitoring, medication management, lifestyle counseling, and coordination with specialists when necessary.

  • Diabetes monitoring and management
  • Hypertension care and monitoring
  • Asthma and COPD management
  • HIV/AIDS care and support
  • Medication reviews and adherence support
Start Care Program

Minor Ailments

We provide prompt and effective treatment for common illnesses and minor health concerns, helping you get back to your daily routine quickly.

From colds and flu to minor injuries and infections, our healthcare team is equipped to diagnose and treat a wide range of everyday health issues.

  • Cold and flu treatment
  • Ear, nose, and throat infections
  • Wound care and dressing
  • Pain management
  • Skin conditions and allergies
Get Treatment

Emergency Care

We provide immediate attention for urgent medical cases during our operating hours. Our team is trained to handle various medical emergencies with speed and expertise.

While we handle many urgent cases, for life-threatening emergencies, we may refer you to the nearest hospital emergency department for specialized care.

  • Immediate injury treatment
  • Acute illness management
  • Allergic reactions
  • Fever and severe pain
  • Emergency referrals when needed
