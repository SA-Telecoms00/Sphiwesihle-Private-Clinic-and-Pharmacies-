Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about booking and our services.

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment online through this page by filling out the booking form, call us at 071 970 9788, or send us a WhatsApp message. We recommend booking online for the fastest confirmation.
What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring your ID, medical aid card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or previous test results. For antenatal visits, bring your maternity certificate if available.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule by calling us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to offer your slot to other patients who may need urgent care.
Do you offer same-day appointments?
Yes, we do offer same-day appointments for urgent cases. Please call us directly at 071 970 9788 or send a WhatsApp message to check availability for same-day bookings.
Is there parking available at the clinic?
Yes, we have ample parking space available for our patients at no additional cost. The parking area is secure and easily accessible.
